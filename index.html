Correlation Between Obesity and Disability

The following chart compares the obesity rate and the rate of adults who are disabled and not in the workforce for each state in the USA.

Analysis

There is a slight correlation between a state's obesity rate and rate of disabled and not in workforce. There are a coupleoutliers that stand out such as North Dakota which has the lowest rate for disabled and not in workforce but a higher obesityrate. On the other end, Puerto Rico has the highest disabled and not in workforce rate but an average obesity rate. As expected,the majority of states that have a high obesity rate also have high disabled and not in workforce rates as seen with states suchas West Virginia, Mississippi, Kentucky, Arkansas and Alabama.
